About Tom

Hi, I’m Tom and I'm a web developer with a passion for iOS, based in the UK. You can view the work I’ve done for clients in my portfolio or read about some of my self published apps and projects. Hopefully on this page you’ll learn more about me. If you have any questions, feel free to get in touch.

I started by developing Wager a betting odds calculator that was released on the App Store in 2010. Shortly after, I developed SITMOS - Podcast App so I could listen to my favourite podcast Stuck in the Middle of Somewhere - which has since been removed from the App Store.

I’ve since gone on to work as a freelance web developer helping a number of clients generate more leads and increase their brand awareness.

I’m currently available, if you have a project you’d like to discuss you can email me tom@tomdiggle.com or take a look at the work I’ve previously done or my apps.

My Apps

If you have any feature requests or support issues with one of my apps then please email tom@tomdiggle.com - I’ll aim to get back to you as soon as possible.

Projects

All of my projects are offered free of charge, are open source and available on GitHub. If you have any suggestions for updates please open an issue on the project’s repository or even better submit a pull request.

About this site

I use Jekyll to build this site, write all the blog posts in Markdown and put the site in a Git repository. The site theme is a custom one, created by me. The fonts used are Museo and Museo Sans by exljbris and the icon font generator is Fontello.

Comments are switched off, head over to Twitter or email me if you want to get in touch about a subject. The site does not contain tracking or analytics of any kind.

Thanks for visiting my site. I’d love to hear your feedback on the site, my writing or portfolio - email tom@tomdiggle.com. You can subscribe to this site via RSS, if you'd like to get notified when I publish a new blog post.
